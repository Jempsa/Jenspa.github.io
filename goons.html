Goons

The Goons are a squad of Rogue Bosses consisting of the following members.

BigPipe

Knight

Birdeye

Notes

  • Birdeyes's footsteps make absolutely no noise.
  • Killing Goons as a Scav does not carry any penalties towards your Scav karma.
  • Goons has different health values than PMCs and Scavs.
  • Instead of the typical 4 single slot pockets, they has 5 double slot pockets, bringing their total space to 10 slots.

Locations

Goons can be found on the following locations:

Customs

At the Scav base (ZB-013 extraction) building with the numerous mounted weapons.

Woods

At the antenna in the northwest (Scav Bunker extraction).

Shoreline

At the weather station on the hill in the southeast.

Lighthouse

At the water treatment plant in the north, and at the blue chalet.

Behavior

When a player is spotted by any one of the Goons, Knight and Big Pipe both rush the player. Birdeye seems to sit back and let Knight and Big Pipe push the opposing enemy first. If they do not succeed and die, he seems to rush the player. His steps are entirely silent. When the goons see you, they'll follow you to the other side of the map, for example, until you're dead.
